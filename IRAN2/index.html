Iran caravanserais

This part of study is focused on Isfahan Province and Fars Province's caravanserais

Isfahan Province

Maranjab caravanserai

Maranjab Caravansarai is a caravanserai in the Maranjab Desert, located at the north of Isfahan province, Iran. It was constructed by the order of Shah Abbas in 1603 and located along the Silk Road, between Khorasan and Isfahan provinces.

Gomrok Caravanserai

Gomrok Caravanserai dates back to the late Qajar period. It was built by Aqa Sharif Fadai. The facades of the travelers' rooms are decorated by strips of tile work, depicting scenes of hunting and festivities.

Soh Caravanserai

Soh Caravansarai is a caravanserai between Isfahan and Kashan, located in Kashan three-way to Moorcheh khort in Isfahan province, Iran. It was constructed in Qajar period and located along the Silk Road.

Jelo gir Caravanserai

Jelo gir Caravanserai belongs to the Safavid period and is located in the city ​​of Najafabad, Damab village.

Zafarghand Caravanserai

This building is located in Zafar Ghand village, 23 km southwest of Ardestan, Isfahan province. It is a two-porch plan and belongs to the Safavid period. This caravanserai is without a tower and the connection of the walls in the four corners are immediate. The entrance of the caravanserai has two small platforms on both sides. The two sides of the porch are divided into two rooms and two small and large arches.

Mahiar Caravanserai

Mahyar Caravanserai belongs to the Safavid period and is located in Shahreza city, central part-Mahyar village. In front of the caravanserai, there is a bazaar, a mosque, a coffee shop and a bakery. These specifications are less common in caravanserais. The rectangular caravanserai is 89 x 82 meters and is built in the shape of four porches.

Gaz Caravanserai

Gaz Caravanserai belongs to the Seljuk period and is located in Borkhar village, Gaz district, north of Isfahan city.

Madar Shah Caravanserai

Madar Shah Caravanserai is one of the historical attractions of Isfahan, which dates back to the Safavid period. This caravanserai is located on the left side of Isfahan-Tehran road and is located at 45 km northwest of Isfahan. The building is 75*86 meters. In the quadrangle of this caravanserai there are four circular towers. The two towers are solid, and the double entrance on either side of the entrance is a hollow that used the interior space as a bathroom.

Chaleh Siah Caravanserai

Chaleh siah (Sheykh Ali khan Zanganeh) Caravanserai was constructed in safavid period. It is located in Jahan Abad village, Isfahan province.The architect of this building was "Professor Taher, son of Professor Reza Isfahani".

Chaharbagh Caravanserai

The foundation of the building of Abbasi Hotel is the caravanserai of Chahar Bagh School. This caravanserai is also called Jeddah and Madarshah and "Sarai Fathiyeh" and is a relic of the last Safavid king who dedicated it to Chaharbagh school. The forty-page endowment of the "New Mubarak Soltani School" [Chaharbagh School] states about this competition: . ” The endowment has made repairing the caravanserai one of the expenses of the endowment. There are not many reports of this caravanserai in Qajar sources, perhaps because the caravanserai was destroyed after the Afghan invasion.

Aminabad Caravanserai

Aminabad Caravanserai belongs to the Safavid period and is located in the south of Isfahan province, 41 km from Shahreza-Shiraz highway and in Shahreza city.

Caravanserais of Fars province

Izadkhast Caravanserai

Izadkhavat Caravanserai is located in the northeast of Izadkhast River and in the north of Abadeh city of Fars province. The interior architecture of this caravanserai, like other caravanserais, is 35 meters long on each side. This caravanserai is located between the historical and ancient city of Abadeh and Isfahan and is one of the most important caravanserais in this region. Izadkhavat Caravanserai is built on a land with an area of ​​4000 meters.

Khan Khoreh Caravanserai

There are very few caravanserais with octagonal design. Khan Khoreh is one of these caravanserais. Khan Khoreh Caravanserai belongs to the Safavid period and is located in Khan Khoreh village, Abadeh city of Shiraz. The choice of octagonal design for the building may be for military and perhaps aesthetic reasons.

Dehbid Caravanserai

Moshir Caravanserai

Moshir Caravanserai belongs to the Qajar period and is located in Shiraz, Qayam Street, Masjed-e-Asalem Alley.

Khani Zanian Caravanserai

Khani Zanian Caravanserai was built in the Qajar era and is known as Moshiri Caravanserai. This caravanserai is located in the middle of Khan Zanian village, 40 km from Shiraz to Bushehr road. This building has four porches and has 4 octagonal towers around the building.

Mian Kothal Caravanserai

Mian Kothal Caravanserai is one of the Safavid buildings in Kazerun . This caravanserai is the only stone caravanserai in Iran and is located in the Barem plain . This caravanserai was built by order of Shah Abbas Safavid and for this reason it is also called Shah Abbasi caravanserai. The total area of ​​this large building, which has a rectangular plan, is about 3,500 square meters, of which 2,500 square meters are the infrastructure of the cells around the central courtyard.

Konar Siah Caravanserai

Kenar Siyah Caravanserai is related to the Sassanian period and are located in Firoozabad city, central part, kenar Siah region. This region is one of the Sassanian and early Islamic periods and is located in the divisions of the country in Firoozabad city in the southwest of Fars province.
